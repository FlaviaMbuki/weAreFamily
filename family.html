Hebrews 11:6
[19]But without faith it is impossible to please him: for he that cometh to God must believe that he is, and that he is a rewarder of them that diligently seek him.
God has loved you so much to the extend He has granted you access to Himself. By acknowledging His love for you, you have the privilege to get to Him. And as sure as He is God, He is a rewarder of them that diligently seek Him, them that come to Him. Prayer is one way of coming to Him. He is a Rewarder. Haleluhyah.

May you be increased in the Grace of our Lord Jesus Christ. Amen. Blessings Beloved.
